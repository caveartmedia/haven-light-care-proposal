Objectives

The business is open. Now the website needs to do its job.

Haven Light Care Limited needs a professional online presence that reassures families, explains services clearly, and makes it easy for people in Coventry to get in touch.

What the website needs to achieve

1
Build local trust and credibility for a new care provider.
2
Clearly present domiciliary care services in a calm, accessible way.
3
Maximise enquiries through call, email and contact form options.
4
Show reviews and community activity to strengthen confidence.
5
Include a future-ready jobs area inside the CMS.

Support needed from us

✓
Copywriting support using the existing mission and vision as the starting point.
✓
Graphics and visual direction using care-focused imagery relevant to the services.
✓
Simple content structure that the three partners can manage easily.
✓
Room to add team information, jobs and community updates over time.
The solution

A focused brochure website built for trust, clarity and local outreach.

The website will be structured around a lean sitemap, strong messaging, clean design, and simple enquiry routes so Haven Light Care Limited can launch quickly and professionally.

Messaging

We will help write and structure the core website copy so visitors can quickly understand the services, values and mission of Haven Light Care Limited.

Design

The visual direction will be clean, warm and professional, using care-related imagery and a reassuring presentation suited to elderly care services.

Functionality

The site will include service information, reviews, contact options, a CMS-backed jobs area, and space to promote community activities and client-focused initiatives.

Investment

A simple fixed-price launch package.
